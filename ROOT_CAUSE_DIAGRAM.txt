┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ CORS HEADER CONFLICT EXPLAINED                                              │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

    ❌ INVALID COMBINATION:
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    
    Access-Control-Allow-Origin: *
    Access-Control-Allow-Credentials: true
    
    Why Invalid:
    • Origin: * means "allow any origin"
    • Credentials: true means "allow credentials"
    • These are mutually exclusive
    • Browser rejects this combination


    ✅ VALID COMBINATIONS:
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    
    Option 1 (No Credentials):
    Access-Control-Allow-Origin: *
    (No Credentials header)
    
    Option 2 (With Credentials):
    Access-Control-Allow-Origin: https://specific-domain.com
    Access-Control-Allow-Credentials: true
