┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 🔴 CRITICAL ISSUE #2: CORS Headers Have Conflicts                           │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  Backend sets:
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  
  res.set('Access-Control-Allow-Origin', '*');
  res.set('Access-Control-Allow-Credentials', 'true');  ← CONFLICT!
  
  
  Why This Fails:
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  
  Browser Rule: These headers are MUTUALLY EXCLUSIVE
  
  ✅ ALLOWED:
     Access-Control-Allow-Origin: *
     (without credentials)
  
  ✅ ALLOWED:
     Access-Control-Allow-Origin: https://specific-domain.com
     Access-Control-Allow-Credentials: true
  
  ❌ NOT ALLOWED:
     Access-Control-Allow-Origin: *
     Access-Control-Allow-Credentials: true
  
  Result: Browser rejects response
